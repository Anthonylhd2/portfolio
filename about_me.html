Overview

Career Summary

I am a junior full-stack developer with nearly two years of experience building real-world applications using technologies like Laravel, Flutter, and PostgreSQL. I’ve developed systems for warehouse management, inventory tracking, and learning platforms, often working closely with non-technical teams to turn requirements into functional software. My strengths include backend development, secure API design, and writing clean, maintainable code. What sets me apart is my drive to solve real-world problems through thoughtful software.

Career Goals

In the short term, I aim to strengthen my frontend skills and gain more hands-on experience with AI and machine learning, especially in ways that can improve efficiency and user experience. Long term, I want to contribute to projects that have a real-world impact — especially in areas like education, accessibility, or digital security. My goal is to keep learning, stay adaptable, and become a developer who builds not just good code, but meaningful solutions.
